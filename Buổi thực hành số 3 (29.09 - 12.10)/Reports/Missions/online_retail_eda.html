  • C mt t l nh gi tr b thiu, ch yu CustomerID.

2. Thng k m t

Phn tch cc ch s trung tm v phn tn cho cc bin s cho thy:

| Bin | ngha | Trung bnh (Mean) | Trung v (Median) | lch chun (Std) | Ghi ch | |------|----------|-------------------|-------------------|---------------------|---------| | Quantity | S lng hng mi dng | Gi tr trung bnh thp nhng lch phi, nhiu gi tr cc ln | Phn ln n hng ch mua t sn phm, vi n c s lng rt ln (bn bun hoc li nhp) | | UnitPrice | n gi sn phm | Trung bnh vi n v tin, nhng c vi gi tr cao bt thng | C kh nng tn ti sn phm cao cp hoc nhp sai gi | | TotalPrice | Tng gi tr tng dng | Phn phi lch phi, mt s n hng c gi tr rt cao | C th do mua s lng ln hoc hng t tin |

Nhn chung, d liu c phn phi lch phi mnh (right-skewed), ngha l phn ln giao dch c gi tr thp, ch vi giao dch to doanh thu cc ln.

3. Doanh thu v sn phm

Phn tch tng doanh thu (TotalPrice) cho thy:

  • Doanh thu tng gim theo tng thng, th hin tnh ma v r rt. Mt vi thng (nh thng l hi, cui nm) ghi nhn doanh thu cao t bin.
  • Top 10 sn phm mang li doanh thu ln nht chim t trng ng k trong tng doanh thu ton b dataset iu ny cho thy t mt hng bn chy chi phi phn ln li nhun (hiu ng Pareto 80/20).
  • Top 10 khch hng v quc gia chim phn ln doanh thu, phn nh tm quan trng ca nhm khch hng trung thnh hoc cc th trng tiu th ln.

5. Phn tch tng quan (Correlation Analysis)

7. Phn tch khch hng (RFM Analysis)

Da trn m hnh RFM (Recency - Frequency - Monetary):

  • Recency: phn ln khch hng khng quay li trong thi gian ngn t l khch hng quay li thp.
  • Frequency: phn ln ch c 12 n hng.
  • Monetary: vi khch hng ng gp doanh thu rt ln (VIP).
    iu ny gi rng siu th nn tng cng chnh sch gi chn khch hng, v d: u i khch hng c, chng trnh tch im.

# Requires InvoiceDate, CustomerID, TotalPrice
snapshot_date = df["InvoiceDate"].max() + pd.Timedelta(days=1)
rfm = df[~df["IsReturn"]].groupby("CustomerID").agg({

    "InvoiceNo": "nunique",
